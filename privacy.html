Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2025

What HmmChk Does

HmmChk is a fact-checking application that analyzes text, images, PDFs, and URLs you share with it to help verify claims and identify potential misinformation.

Information We Process

When you use HmmChk, the content you submit (text, images, PDFs, or URLs) is sent to Google's Gemini API for analysis. This content is processed solely to provide you with fact-checking results and is not stored by HmmChk after your session ends.

Third-Party Services

HmmChk uses Google's Gemini API to perform analysis. Your submitted content is processed according to Google's privacy policy and terms of service. We recommend reviewing Google's AI privacy practices at https://ai.google.dev/terms.

Data We Do Not Collect

HmmChk does not:

  • Create user accounts
  • Store your submitted content after analysis
  • Collect personal information
  • Track your location
  • Use cookies or analytics
  • Share data with advertisers

Data Retention

HmmChk does not retain the content you submit. Once your fact-check results are delivered, your submitted content is not stored on our servers.

Children's Privacy

HmmChk is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly process content from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
